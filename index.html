About Me

Hello, I'm Nitesh Yadav

I'm a visual artist from Modinagar, deeply passionate about expressing stories through digital and charcoal art. My journey with art began in childhood, and since then, it has become a powerful way for me to share my thoughts, emotions, and imagination with the world.

Much of my inspiration comes from dreamsâ€”those strange, vivid moments that live between sleep and reality. I love turning those abstract ideas into powerful visual pieces that invite viewers to explore their own imagination and connect with the artwork on a personal level.

Through my art, I strive to create a bridge between the conscious and subconscious, bringing dreamlike elements into reality through careful attention to detail and emotional depth in every piece I create.

What Inspires Me

My art is deeply influenced by:

  • The ever-changing moods of nature and landscapes
  • Human emotions and the complexity of relationships
  • Urban environments and architectural elements
  • Literature, poetry, and music that evoke strong imagery
  • The interplay of light, shadow, and color in everyday moments
